About Us

The Cafe was established to provide the best coffee using the highest quality roasted beans available. Our selection uses only naturally grown beans from countries across the world.

Our selection will change from time to time so keep this in mind. We will give advanced notice prior to any change.

With this in mind our selection may be small but our coffee is top notch. To compliment our fine coffee selection are cakes and other pastries.

To go along with your favorite coffee and cake or brownies. Feel free to sit and relax with a book or any of your mobile devices using our free WiFi or Ethernet connections.
